About Judah's House Of Worship

Judah's House Of Worship (JHOW), also known as The Representers Of Christ: is a vibrant family of believers devoted to worship, prayer, the word, and a walk with God. We are rooted in love, led by the Spirit, and raised by God to impact generations.
